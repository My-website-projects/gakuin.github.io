Vision

To be the leading Japanese Agency in Sri Lanka, dedicated to Enriching Sri Lankan students' lives by enabling them to pursue higher education in Japan.

Mision

To provide comprehensive Japanese language education, combined with expert guidance and support, to equip Sri Lankan students with the proficiency and cultural understanding required for successful admission into esteemed Japanese universities and institutions while providing part-time jobs and lodging, fostering their academic and personal growth.

Director's Messages

In 2007, I founded R&S International Gakuin (Pvt) Latd. with a clear vision of offering opportunities for Sri Lankan students to migrate to Japan and thrive in their chosen fields. I started by teaching the Japanese language, focusing on helping my students pass the NAT/JLPT Better Language Knowledge and well decepline. Witnessing the positive impact and success of my students, I realized the immense potential to expand our services and providecomprehensive support for higher education in Japan. Over the years, R&S International Gakuin Pvt Ltd has grown exponentially, with the support of an expanded director board inclusive of a Japanese National. Today, we take pride in sending students from Sri Lanka to language schools in various cities across Japan.
